Our Story

Welcome to our family-run baking business! We are a small Irish family who has always had a passion for baking delicious treats. Our love for baking started in our home kitchen and has now grown into a small business where customers can order our baked goods online.

Our goal is to bring joy to your taste buds and sweeten up your day with our delectable creations. From traditional Irish scones and soda bread to indulgent brownies and cookies, we have something for every sweet tooth. All of our recipes are made with the freshest ingredients and are baked with love and care.

Thank you for choosing to support our small business. We hope you enjoy our baked goods as much as we enjoy making them for you.

Our Kitchen

Our small family business kitchen is a warm and welcoming place, where the smells of freshly baked goods fill the air and the sound of happy conversation and laughter can often be heard. It is a place where a close-knit team of family members work together to create delicious treats that are sold online.

Our Ingreidents

We at Treat Yourself are passionate about using the freshest and finest ingredients in their creations. They source their flour, sugar, eggs, butter, and other ingredients from local farms and producers, ensuring that their products are made with the highest-quality ingredients.
